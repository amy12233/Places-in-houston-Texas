In the heart of Sugar Land, Town Square offers international dining choices, lively events and specialty shopping that includes The Warehouse, Twenty-Two Fifty Interiors, and The Loft.

Although there's enough shopping here to keep you busy throughout the weekend, that's not all you can do in Sugar Land Town Square. Spoil yourself with a massage and a manicure and pedicure at Venetian Nail Spa or work up a sweat at Revolution Studio or the Yes Yoga Studio's classes.
